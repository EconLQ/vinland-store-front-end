Welcome to Vinland Store

Your one-stop shop for all things intriguing, thought-provoking, and occasionally absurd. Think of us as the Craig Jones of online content—always the 2nd best, but somehow way more entertaining.

What We Do At Vinland Store

We specialize in serving up a delightful mix of blogs, discussions, and random musings on whatever the author finds interesting. Whether it's deep dives into obscure historical events, hot takes on the latest trends in tech, or just a little bit of humor, we've got you covered.

Why Vinland Store?

  • Craig Jones Approved: While we can't claim to be the best (that title's taken), we’re proud to be the 2nd best at what we do—just like Craig. And let’s be honest, being 2nd means we’re still pretty darn good.
  • No Boring Stuff: We promise to keep things fresh, thought-provoking, and occasionally ridiculous.

Comments:

Anyone can comment here, but spam and trollers may be removed or banned. Comments should be polite, on topic, short (< 500 words), and sparing with quotations (links are ok). Don’t repeat your own comments word for word. We have Open Threads once a month for general discussion; longer comments are acceptable there. Commenting frequency: A good rule of thumb is that your name should not appear more than two times in the 10 most recent comments, as shown on the right sidebar. Three times is acceptable on rare occasions. Four times, never. Post authors are of course excepted. To help us enforce this, we ask that you only use one name when commenting here.
